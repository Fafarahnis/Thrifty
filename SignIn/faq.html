How Can We Help?

Find answers to common questions about our car rental services

Booking Questions

To rent a car with us, you'll need:

  • A valid driver's license (held for at least 1 year)
  • A credit card in the renter's name
  • Proof of insurance (or purchase our coverage)
  • Minimum age of 21 (additional fees under 25)

We require a credit card in the renter's name for all reservations. Debit cards may be accepted at some locations for payment, but a credit card is still required to secure the booking and for the security deposit.

You can modify or cancel your reservation:

  • Online through your account
  • Via our mobile app
  • By calling 1-800-RENT-CAR

Cancellations made more than 24 hours before pickup are free. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

Rental Policies

Our standard policy is "full-to-full":

  • You receive the car with a full tank
  • Return it with a full tank
  • If returned with less fuel, we'll charge for the missing fuel plus a service fee

We also offer prepaid fuel options at discounted rates.

Additional drivers must:

  • Be at least 21 years old
  • Present a valid license at the counter
  • Be listed on the rental agreement

There's a small daily fee for each additional driver (spouses may drive free with documentation).
